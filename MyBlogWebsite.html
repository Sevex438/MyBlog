My Gamedev vlog #2

Set on a picturesque island akin to Stardew Valley, this game blends charming community life with intense deception. Players engage in daily activities like farming, fishing, and crafting while interacting with around 12 unique residents. As night falls, the game shifts to a darker tone, where players must identify a doppelganger—an entity that can shapeshift into any resident. Observation of residents’ behavior, actions, and speech during the day is crucial to spotting these impostors. Incorrectly identifying a doppelganger and sleeping leads to death, while staying awake results in a confrontation with the doppelganger. Survive the nights, thrive during the day, and use your skills to navigate this suspenseful and engaging world.
